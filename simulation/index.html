Verification of Millman's Theorem

Procedure:

Keep all the resistances (R1, R2, & R3) close to their respective maximum values. Choose any arbitrary values of V1, V2 and V3.

Experiment Part Select:

Case 1:
Select switches S1 and S2 to power and S3 to load. Simulate the program. Observe the value of the load current (IL) from Case 1 tab.

Case-2:
a)Millman Voltage analysis:
Apply switches S1 and S2 to power and S3 to intermediate. Simulate the program. Read Millman voltage (Vth) from Case 2(a) tab.

b)Millman Resistance analysis:
Apply switches S1 and S2 to short and S3 to power. Simulate the program. Read Millman resistance (Rth) from Case 2(b) tab.

Case-3: Using Vth and Rth determine Load Current:
Specify the load resistance in case of the result table as the same load resistance entered in the main circuit. Simulate the program. Read Load current (IL) from Case 3 tab. Compare the load current (IL) obtained from Case 1.
N.B.:- All the resistances are in ohms.
